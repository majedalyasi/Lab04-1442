Introduction

The city of Riyadh is an Arab Islamic city located in the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia, and represents its administrative capital and its largest city. The city has been built on a site where people have lived since ancient times. Some stone tools were found that date back to the Paleolithic Age, and it is believed that they were built over the city of (Hajar), which reached its height in pre-Islamic times and the beginning of the Islamic era. However, the transfer of the center of government from the Arabian Peninsula to Damascus and Baghdad, The city had lost its status and importance, then the name (Riyadh) began to appear during the reign of Imam (Turki bin Abdullah) when he made it the capital of the second Saudi state in 1824 CE, and the city was subjected in the following years to many wars and conflicts, until it was ruled by the king (Abd Aziz Al Saud) in 1902 AD, and continued to wage military campaigns until finally achieving independence, and thus the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia was established, and the city of Riyadh was chosen as the official capital of the Kingdom in 1932 AD, and it was called Riyadh; Because it is famous for its orchards and palm trees in a dry and arid environment.

Geographical location and area

The city of Riyadh is located in the middle of the Arabian Peninsula, specifically in the eastern part of the Najd plateau, and is surrounded by Saudi cities from every direction. From the west, the city of Dammam is located at a distance of about 389 km, and it is 1245 km from the city of Jizan to the northeast, and it is 880 km from the holy city of Mecca to the east, and it is from the city of Hail at a distance of 641 km to the southeast, and the city rises about 600 meters above the level of the surface The sea, as for astronomers, the city of Riyadh is located on a latitude between 34-38 degrees to the north, and a longitude between 43-46 degrees to the east, [2] and in view of the geographical area, the city of Riyadh has undergone a major shift in its area; It was initially a small town surrounded by walls from every direction, then later it became a modern city with wide borders, according to geographical studies, the area of ​​the city of Riyadh was about 1782 km2, and thus it is nearly three times the size of Bahrain, or Singapore.

CSS

CSS is an abbreviation for Cascading Style Sheet, which means flowing style sheets, and CSS technology is used in designing web pages so that the entire look of the site is controlled in terms of the type, color, or size of the font used, without the need to write those codes or repeat them in Each page within the site, and therefore it is possible to make any modifications to all files through only one file, which saves a lot of effort and time. The function of this technology is to control how the web page is displayed without interfering with the content, which facilitates the process of managing the site, modifying a single CSS file will affect the design of all website pages. CSS files simplify the design and management of sites, and solve many problems that any website developer may face, for example if you have designed your site using HTML, and assuming that this site consists of five pages, then you want after a while Developing and modifying the way this site is displayed ... Here you have to redesign the five pages again, this is very easy and you have to start now. Assuming that this site contains a greater number of pages, and let it be 50 or 100 pages, it is sure that you will Hesitate before embarking on the development and modification ... But if you have designed your site from the beginning using CSS technology, then all you have to do is modify one file only.
